FAQ

Q: Does DaalBot have paywalls
A: No, DaalBot has no paywalls.

Q: Why are logs not working?
A: It could be caused by DaalBot not having acces to the channel logs are being sent to.

Q: Why is DaalBot offline?
A: DaalBot could be offline for a number of reasons, the most common being that the host lost power or a unrecoverable error occured.
